What is Silent Hill?

it's a series of survival horror video games produced by the Japanese company Konami, the first four parts are developed by "Team Silent"

The Beginning Of Silent Hill

Silent Hill dates back to the late sixteenth century until 1976, that is, before Christopher Columbus discovered the American continent. , people have always felt a strong spiritual energy in the place of that town, and after that the town was met with a deadly epidemic that claimed many lives, and in 1800 Silent Hill was made a mining colony and prisons were built there, especially for prisoners of war. Then, after the war, an outlaw named "Jeb Foster" arrived in the town with his pregnant wife, and soon began to see the ghosts of his bad past, and among the series of "Jeb" crimes was his murder of a newborn child, and soon the mother of that child was trying to steal his child in the form of a ghost,thus far the townspeople began to disappear at random suspiciously.

With the beginning of the nineteenth century, the town of Silent Hill became a tourist area due to its nature and picturesque lakes, yet many tourists found mysterious drawings on the stones in the deep mines, and rumors began to spread about seeing terrifying monsters, and ships and visitors disappeared without a trace in the town's lake known as “Lake Toluca”, and the beginning of the story of the first part chronologically of the series was called Silent Hill: Origins, which was released in 2007 on the PlayStation 2

Silent Hill: Origins

He also learns about the mysterious sect known as the “Order”, which aims to sacrifice “Alyssa” to create the body of their master they serve. He also discovers a dark dimension of reality that lies in the city of Silent Hill known as the other world, And after his final confrontation with the last monster and his defeat, the magic tool that Travis collects its parts during the events of the game splits Elissa's soul into two parts, and a newborn baby appears above Elisa's chest,

which deprives the cult of performing the ritual permanently, and at the end of the events The Game Travis flees Silent Hill and catches one last glimpse of Alyssa holding the baby in her arms.

Silent Hill 1999

As for the first part of the series, Silent Hill, it was released in 1999 on the PlayStation 1, and it was later published on the network,The events of Silent Hill 1 begin in 1983, where the hero of the game “Harry Mason” and his wife find a baby girl on the road and give her the name “Sheryl”, knowing that this child is the same one that separates from “Alice” in the previous part.

Seven years later, Harry loses His wife grieves deeply, and Sheryl persuades him in some way to take a vacation in the town of Silent Hill, and they go together to the town where Harry suffers a collision on the road. When he wakes up, he finds himself alone without Sheryl, and he goes to search for her in the deserted and foggy town, but suddenly He hears sirens and the town begins to transform until he finds himself in a terrifying otherworldly world.

At this stage, Silent Hill has become full of monsters and dangerous creatures without any trace of humans. Harry is forced to make his way on a perilous and suspenseful tour in search of Sherrill. The last monster escapes with the child through a portal to return to the real world, and it appears later that this infant is the heroine of the third part of the series, which is called “Heather”, and Sheryl disappears forever after she reunited with Elisa, who was separated from her since Silent Hill: Origins.

Silent Hill 2

We will talk here about the second part of the Silent Hill series, which was released on the PlayStation 2 and Xbox 360 in 2001, while the PC version only got a special version in 2002. Silent Hill 2 events take place in 1993,

ten years after the events of the previous part. It begins after the main character “James” receives a letter from his deceased wife, “Mary”, inviting him to return to Silent Hill to meet her there. Maria is a woman who appears to be a more active mirror of his late wife, who will die in front of him many times to relive the painful experience of losing his wife again and again,

and “James” is forced in the end to confront the feeling of guilt that overwhelms him as a result of ending the life of his terminally ill wife, which manifests itself in the form of A flying monster is defeated by “James”, before he sees the ghost of his wife “Mary” and apologizes to her for what he did and then leaves town. It is worth noting that this part has multiple endings.

Silent Hill 3

We come to the third part of the Silent Hill series, which was released for the PlayStation 2 in 2003, and a version for the PC was released in the same year. The game takes place in the year 2000.

We all remember the baby that “Alyssa” gave to “Harry” at the end of the first part of the Silent series. Hill, that baby girl is actually the heroine of the third part called “Heather”, who is 17 years old, and who has always had dreams related to the town of Silent Hill and monsters that kill her every time. Her story begins with this ill-fated town when “Heather” goes to The subway train to return home and check on her father. After arriving home, Heather is surprised by the body of her murdered father on the couch.

Traces of blood follow all the way to the roof of the building to find one of the priests of the Order sect. What he did in Silent Hill 1, “Heather” then swears revenge on the Order Sect for what she did to her father, and goes to the town of Silent Hill with the help of a detective to carry out her revenge, and in the end discovers that she is a copy of the other “Alyssa”, and “Heather” is forced to confront Mr. Which the sect has been trying to create since the beginning of the series and defeats him after a long struggle.

Silent Hill 4: The Room

As for the fourth part of the Silent Hill series, titled The Room, it was released in 2004 on PlayStation 2, Xbox 360, and PC. As for the PlayStation 3 version, it was only released on the Japanese PlayStation Store.

The game takes place one year after the events of the previous part, and it revolves around the hero of the game, “Henry Townshend,” who finds himself trapped in his room, which is located in the south of the town of “Ashfield” adjacent to the city of Silent Hill, and after Several days Henry discovers a mysterious hole in the wall of his bathroom that actually leads to the other world,

and after entering this gate he witnesses the brutal killing of a number of victims who bear strange numbers on their heads or chests, then Henry meets the child “Waltersullivan”, who grew up in the Silent Hill orphanage, And he learned the religion and rituals of the Order sect,

and despite killing himself, his spirit remained alive in the place, killing in order to complete the rituals that would give life to the evil that resides in the room, “Walter” then attacks “Henry” neighbor “Eileen” and kidnaps her, and “Henry” goes To the other world to search for her in the hospital and save her in the end, and then the two decide after that to explore the spirit of “Walter” to find before them many connected worlds in the other world, and they are forced to confront and defeat the ghosts of the previous victims of “Walter”, who is actually a dead serial killer In the next room in the other world.

Silent Hill: Homecoming

Here we will talk about the new part of the series called Homecoming, which was released in 2008 on PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, Xbox 1 and PC in 2012.

It takes place in 2007, and revolves around its hero, “Alex Shepherd”, who returns to his home after recovering from He suffered severe injuries on the battlefield in order to search for his disappeared brother, and his search will lead him to his village in New England and then he will end up in the city of horror and mystery Silent Hill, where he will find himself amidst strange events taking place in the town hospital such as children drowning or being brutally killed in front of him, Later,

he will discover that these children are the ones who were sacrificed during the war, and their parents tortured and suffocated them in the hospital, and the children in the game will take revenge on them in the form of angry monsters, and he will discover more about the past of the Order cult and the town of Silent Hill.

Silent Hill: P.T.

It is worth noting that Konami tried to revive the series in cooperation with the famous creator Kojima in 2014, as it launched an initial teaser demo for the project that was under construction under the name P.T. Unfortunately...

a problem occurred and the project was stopped, most likely due to a change in Konami's plan within the company regarding In video games, it no longer gives priority to games with a high rating of AAA, and it focuses on small games, especially smart device games, and on its business and investments away from the field of games.

Of course, this was a shock to many of the administrative names specialized in huge games within the company and prompted them to resign and leave it, and only Kojima was left. The fate of the series no longer seemed clear at that time, and this is probably the main reason why we did not see any remake of the games of the series, although the first parts of it got Good reviews.
